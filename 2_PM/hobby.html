My Hobbies!

Chinese yo-yo

The Chinese yo-yo, also known as the diabolo, is a traditional form of performing arts in ancient China. It is the most common of all the traditional Chinese toys, and it is a toy that I have played and performed with for eight years as of today. Here's a video I made with a few friends last year while we were suffering of boredom in the summer months of 2020. (I'm the one in blue!)
