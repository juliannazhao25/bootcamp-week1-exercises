Favorite Book

The Last Lecture

A book I've loved since sixth grade, The Last Lecture both brings me a million memories and new experiences with each reread. But why exactly is it my favorite? Well...

Some reasons:

  1. A sense of humor that never fails to give me lots of giggles
  2. Life lessons that are actually worth listening to
  3. Showed me how cool it would be to grow up into a CS person just like the author :)
